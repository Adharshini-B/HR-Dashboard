2. Short Description
            An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze employee demographics, workforce distribution, attrition trends, and key HR metrics. The dashboard helps organizations make informed HR decisions through data visualization and business intelligence.

            This Power BI dashboard provides valuable insights into employee data by analyzing attrition, department performance, gender distribution, salary, education, and workforce demographics using interactive reports and visualizations.

3. Tech Stack
        -Power BI Desktop – Dashboard Development
        -Microsoft Excel – Data Source
        -Power Query – Data Cleaning & Transformation
        -DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated Measures & KPIs
        -Data Visualization – Interactive Charts, Cards, Maps & Slicers

4. Data Source
            The dashboard is developed using a sample HR employee dataset containing workforce information such as employee demographics, department, job role, salary, education, years at company, and attrition status.

Dataset Includes:
          Employee ID
          Age
          Gender
          Department
          Job Role
          Education Field
          Marital Status
          Monthly Income
          Years at Company
          Attrition
          Job Satisfaction
          Business Travel

5. Features / Highlights
Business Problem
            Human Resource departments often manage large volumes of employee data, making it difficult to identify workforce trends, employee attrition, and performance indicators. This dashboard provides a centralized view of key HR metrics to support informed decision-making.

Dashboard Features
          Employee Overview Dashboard
          Attrition Analysis
          Department-wise Employee Distribution
          Gender Diversity Analysis
          Age Group Analysis
          Education Field Analysis
          Salary Distribution
          Job Role Analysis
          Interactive Filters & Slicers
          KPI Cards for HR Metrics
          
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
          Total Employees
          Active Employees
          Attrition Count
          Attrition Rate
          Average Employee Age
          Average Monthly Income
          Average Years at Company
          
Key Insights
          Identifies departments with the highest employee attrition.
          Analyzes workforce distribution across different business units.
          Compares employee demographics by age and gender.
          Evaluates salary distribution among employees.
          Tracks employee retention and turnover trends.
          Supports HR professionals in workforce planning and strategic decision-making.
          
Business Impact
          Enables HR teams to monitor employee turnover effectively.
          Improves workforce planning through data-driven insights.
Helps identify high-risk attrition groups.
Supports employee retention strategies.
Provides interactive reports for faster business decision-making.
